# Sports

## Pro teams
- **Milwaukee Bucks** (NBA) — Fiserv Forum, downtown Deer District. 2021 NBA champions (Giannis era).
- **Milwaukee Brewers** (MLB) — American Family Field (formerly Miller Park). Retractable roof. Tailgating culture.
- **Milwaukee Admirals** (AHL hockey) — UW-Milwaukee Panther Arena.
- **Milwaukee Wave** (MASL indoor soccer) — Panther Arena.

## Not in MKE but locally worshipped
- **Green Bay Packers** (NFL) — Green Bay, ~2 hr north. Many MKE locals are season-ticket waitlisted for decades.
- **Wisconsin Badgers** (college football/basketball) — Madison, ~90 min west.

## College
- **Marquette Golden Eagles** (Big East basketball) — Fiserv Forum shared home.
- **UW-Milwaukee Panthers** (Horizon League).

## Outdoor / participation
- Lakefront running + biking: Oak Leaf Trail (100+ mi county-wide network).
- Winter: ice skating at Red Arrow Park (downtown), skiing at Alpine Valley (1 hr), Crystal Ridge (Franklin).
- Summer: Lake Michigan sailing, kayaking Milwaukee River, Bradford Beach volleyball.
- Fishing: Lake Michigan salmon/trout charters from Milwaukee harbor.

## Tailgating notes
- American Family Field tailgating opens hours before first pitch; grills, lots of beer. It's a ritual, not just a parking strategy.
- Klement's Famous Racing Sausages (6th-inning mascot race) — pick your favorite early.
