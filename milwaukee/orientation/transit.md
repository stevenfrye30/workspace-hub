# Transit & getting around

## Primary modes
- **Car** — the default. Milwaukee is a car city; most destinations assume one.
- **MCTS (Milwaukee County Transit System)** — bus network. MCTS Connect is the BRT line (Wauwatosa ↔ Downtown).
- **The Hop** — free downtown streetcar. Runs Third Ward ↔ Intermodal Station ↔ East Side. Nice for downtown hops.
- **Bublr Bikes** — bike share. Good weather only.
- **Walking** — viable inside Third Ward, East Side, Bay View, Walker's Point, Tosa Village, Brady St.

## Highways
- **I-94** — east/west (to Chicago south, Madison west).
- **I-43** — north/south (to Green Bay north, Beloit south).
- **I-894** — beltway around the south side.
- **US-41 / I-41** — north to Fond du Lac / Green Bay.

Marquette Interchange (I-94 / I-43 / I-794) is the main downtown junction.

## Intercity
- **Amtrak Hiawatha** — Milwaukee ↔ Chicago, ~90 min, ~7x daily. Intermodal Station downtown.
- **Mitchell International Airport (MKE)** — south side. Reasonably sized hub. Free cell phone lot.
- **Coach USA / Megabus / Badger Bus** — Madison + Chicago routes.

## Parking
- Downtown metered + garages. Residential permit parking in some East Side / Bay View streets.
- Winter overnight parking rules (alternate-side) apply during snow emergencies — check MKE parking rules before first snow.
