# Food & drink

## Must-experience categories
- **Frozen custard** — it's not ice cream. Kopp's, Leon's, Gilles, Culver's (chain but WI-born). Daily flavor rotations.
- **Friday fish fry** — cod or perch, potato pancake or fries, rye bread, coleslaw. Lakefront Brewery's is famous + includes a polka band. Kegel's Inn (Tosa) is classic.
- **Cheese curds** — fresh (squeaky) or fried. Look for the squeak.
- **Bratwurst** — Usinger's is the benchmark. Klement's too.
- **Butter burgers** — Solly's, AJ Bombers, Culver's.
- **Cream puffs** — State Fair cream puffs are a summer ritual (Aug).
- **Supper clubs** — order a brandy Old Fashioned, get the relish tray, order prime rib or fish. Five O'Clock Steakhouse (city option), Mason Street Grill.

## Coffee
- Colectivo (local chain, several cafés).
- Stone Creek Coffee.
- Anodyne Coffee Roasters (Bay View).

## Notable restaurants
- **Sanford** — East Side fine dining, longtime institution.
- **Ardent** — tasting menu, Brady area.
- **Lazy Susan** — Bay View, New American.
- **Odd Duck** — Bay View, globally-influenced.
- **Bavette La Boucherie** — butcher + sandwiches + wine, Third Ward.
- **Braise** — farm-to-table, Walker's Point.

## Groceries
- **Outpost Natural Foods** — co-op (Riverwest, Bay View, Capitol, Wauwatosa).
- **Sendik's** — upscale local chain.
- **Metro Market / Pick 'n Save** — Kroger family.
- **Woodman's** — big-box value (Tosa, Oak Creek).
- **Fresh Thyme**, **Whole Foods** (Mayfair area).

## Drink
- Beer is obviously central. Miller/MillerCoors HQ is here; PBR revived local brewery; plus craft (Lakefront, MKE Brewing, Good City, Third Space, Eagle Park, Broken Bat, Enlightened).
- Brandy: Wisconsin consumes a huge share of US brandy, largely via Old Fashioneds. Korbel is common.
