# Utilities & setup

## Electric & gas
- **We Energies** (wisconsinelectric/WE) — monopoly utility for electric + natural gas in the MKE area.
  - Set up: we-energies.com or 1-800-242-9137. Deposits sometimes required for new accounts without WI credit history.
  - Budget billing option smooths winter gas spikes.

## Water / sewer
- **Milwaukee Water Works** (City of Milwaukee) — handled through the city. Suburbs have their own (e.g., Wauwatosa Water).
- Typically billed quarterly.

## Internet
- **Spectrum** (cable) — widely available.
- **AT&T** (fiber in many areas, DSL elsewhere).
- **T-Mobile / Verizon home 5G** — valid backup depending on address.
- Check availability by specific address — varies block to block.

## Trash / recycling
- City of Milwaukee: provided, cart-based. Check pickup day + rules on mkedpw.milwaukee.gov.
- Suburbs contract out — usually weekly with separate recycling.
- Yard waste: seasonal schedule; leaves get collected in fall.

## Other
- **Renters insurance** — strongly advised; ~$15/mo. Many landlords require.
- **Homeowners insurance** — factor ice dam / basement water coverage.
- **TV** — most cut the cord. Free over-the-air gets local CBS (58), NBC (4), ABC (12), Fox (6), PBS (10/36).

## Setup day-of checklist
- [ ] We Energies account active
- [ ] Water transferred / in your name
- [ ] Internet installed (schedule 2+ weeks ahead if possible)
- [ ] Mailbox key (for buildings with cluster boxes)
- [ ] Smoke/CO detectors tested
- [ ] Furnace filter checked
