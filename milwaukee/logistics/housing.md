# Housing search

## Rent benchmarks (approximate, 2026 dollars — verify before committing)
- **Studio / 1BR** in Third Ward / East Side / Bay View: ~$1,400–2,200
- **1BR** in Riverwest / Walker's Point: ~$1,100–1,700
- **2BR** in Shorewood / Tosa: ~$1,500–2,400
- **Suburbs (West Allis, South Milwaukee)**: generally lower

## Where to search
- Apartments.com, Zillow, Trulia (standard)
- Craigslist (still active, smaller landlords)
- Facebook Marketplace + neighborhood groups
- Local brokers: Mandel Group, Ogden & Company, Katz Properties

## Key lease factors in MKE
- **Parking**: included? On-street permit? Heated garage (matters in winter).
- **Heat**: included in rent or tenant-paid? Radiator vs. forced air. Winter bills can be $150–300/mo if tenant-paid.
- **Laundry**: in-unit, in-building, none? "None" is common in older buildings.
- **Lake access**: "lake view" often means "if you lean out the window." Verify.
- **Age of building**: lots of pre-war brick — charming but can have ventilation, lead, or insulation quirks.
- **Snow removal**: who shovels? Small landlords often push it on tenants.

## If buying
- Property taxes in WI are high relative to income tax (opposite of, e.g., IL). Budget accordingly.
- Winter inspections: check roof condition, ice dam history, basement moisture.
- Historic neighborhoods (Historic Third Ward, Prospect Ave) may have restrictions.

## Short-term landing pad options
If move-in timing is messy:
- Extended-stay hotels near downtown
- Short-term furnished rentals (Landing, Blueground, Furnished Finder)
- Airbnb month-to-month in Bay View / East Side for a reconnaissance month
