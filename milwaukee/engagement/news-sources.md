# News sources

## Daily / breaking
- **Milwaukee Journal Sentinel** (jsonline.com) — paper of record; print + digital. Paywall.
- **TMJ4** (NBC) — local TV news; solid web site.
- **WTMJ 620 AM** — news/talk radio.
- **WISN 12** (ABC), **CBS 58**, **FOX6** — other TV stations, each with sites.

## Alternative / long-form / civic
- **Urban Milwaukee** (urbanmilwaukee.com) — development, policy, transit, culture. Excellent local accountability reporting. Paywall-lite.
- **OnMilwaukee** (onmilwaukee.com) — culture, restaurants, events. Lighter, lifestyle-oriented.
- **Milwaukee Magazine** — monthly + web; dining, features.
- **Shepherd Express** — alt-weekly, progressive, arts coverage.
- **Wisconsin Examiner / Wisconsin Watch** — state-level investigative.

## Public radio
- **WUWM 89.7** — NPR affiliate; local show "Lake Effect" weekdays 10 AM.
- **Radio Milwaukee 88.9** — indie / local music; also runs cultural journalism.

## TV
- **Milwaukee PBS** — channels 10 / 36.

## Neighborhood / hyperlocal
- **Milwaukee Neighborhood News Service** (milwaukeenns.org) — community-focused, historically under-covered areas.
- **Bay View Compass**, **Riverwest Currents** — neighborhood papers.
- Facebook neighborhood groups (search "Bay View Neighbors" etc.).
- Nextdoor (use cautiously — high noise).

## Newsletters to consider
- Milwaukee Journal Sentinel's daily briefing
- Urban Milwaukee's "The Recap"
- Bublr / Visit Milwaukee (events)
