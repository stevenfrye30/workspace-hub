## Weekly / monthly recurring
- **Gallery Night & Day** — Third Ward, quarterly Fri/Sat.
- **Jazz in the Park** — Thursdays, Cathedral Square, summer.
- **River Rhythms** — Wednesdays, Pere Marquette Park, summer.
- **Farmers markets** — Tosa (Sat), South Shore (Sat, Bay View), West Allis (Sat), Fondy (Sat/Tue, near north side), Riverwest Gardeners Market (Sun).
