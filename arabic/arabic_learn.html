الأرقام — Numbers

Arabic uses Eastern Arabic numerals (٠١٢٣…) in most Arab countries, though Western numerals are also common. Note: Arabic numbers are written left to right, even though the rest of text flows right to left. There is also a gender agreement rule: numbers 3–10 take the opposite gender of the noun they count.

Grammar Essentials

Arabic is a root-based language. Most words derive from a 3-letter (sometimes 4-letter) root. Mastering roots multiplies your vocabulary rapidly.
