Arabic builds thousands of words from 3-letter roots. Learn the root and a handful of patterns and you can guess new words. Click any derived word to hear it.

The article "the" is written ال (al-), but its pronunciation depends on the next letter. Before a sun letter the "l" assimilates (الشمس is "ash-shams", not "al-shams"). Before a moon letter the "l" stays.

☀ Sun letters — 14

"L" assimilates into the next consonant (doubles it). Spelling unchanged; pronunciation changes.

🌙 Moon letters — 14

"L" stays audible: "al-" sounds exactly as written.
